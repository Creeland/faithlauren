bcrypt says yes or no

src/auth.ts → authorize()

The Darkroom

Where uploaded photos are developed.

src/app/api/uploadthing/core.ts
›

Photos never touch the server's disk — they go to UploadThing. Three routers (gallery, portfolio, group cover) each verify admin, insert in a transaction at the next sortOrder, revalidate pages, then backfill dimensions with sharp, fire-and-forget.

What lives here
  • api/uploadthing/core.tsThe FileRouter: galleryPhoto, portfolioPhoto, groupCoverImage.
  • api/uploadthing/route.tsWires the router into a route handler.
  • lib/uploadthing.ts + hooks/use-upload-queue.tsClient helpers + a resilient upload queue (tested).
How it flows
1
.middleware() rejects anyone whose role isn't ADMIN.
2
File lands in the cloud; .onUploadComplete() fires.
3
A $transaction inserts at max(sortOrder)+1; pages revalidate.
4
import('sharp') lazily reads width/height and updates the row — unawaited.
